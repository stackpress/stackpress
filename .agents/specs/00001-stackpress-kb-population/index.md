## Purpose

This spec plans the rebuild of `.agents/context/` after purging the initial
docs-shaped package KB. The rebuilt KB should help future agents produce
documentation, articles, diagrams, teaching material, brand and marketing
messages, and contributor guidance from Stackpress's design intent.

## Current State

- Spec status: Frozen on 2026-07-11 after document-level parity.
- Context state: eight accepted technical files plus 17 linked references are
  promoted and retrieval-validated.
- Maintenance state: detailed-layer and document-regression rules are routed
  from `.agents/AGENTS.md` and compatibility context.
- Phase 8 state: all 47 documents have KB-only structured records; eight major
  domains have prose drafts; benchmark drift and source conflict are classified.

## Promotion Rule

Do not promote strategy, unverified interpretation, raw source notes, or
one-phase tasks into `.agents/context/`. Promote only accepted reusable truth
after the research loop produces source-backed concepts and resolves material
gaps.
